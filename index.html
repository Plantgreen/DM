Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our digital marketing course

Is there really a 100% placement guarantee?

Yes! We offer 100% placement assurance for our Pro and Premium plans to all students who complete the course requirements, including assignments and projects. We have partnerships with 300+ companies who hire our students for internships and full-time roles. In the rare case that we're unable to place you within 3 months of course completion, we'll refund 50% of your course fee.

Do I need any prior experience in marketing?

No prior experience is required. Our course is designed for complete beginners as well as professionals looking to upskill. We start with the fundamentals and gradually progress to advanced concepts, ensuring everyone can follow along and gain practical skills.

What's the duration of the course?

The core program consists of 18 sessions with 4-6 hours of live sessions per week. However, you'll have lifetime access to all course materials and can learn at your own pace if needed. Most students spend 10-12 hours per week including live sessions, assignments, and projects.

What payment options do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI payments (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), net banking, and EMI options. You can also pay in installments if needed - just contact our support team for arrangements.

Will I get a certificate after completion?

Yes, upon successful completion of all course requirements (attendance, assignments, and final project), you'll receive a verifiable digital certificate that's recognized by industry partners. This certificate can be shared on LinkedIn and included in your resume.
